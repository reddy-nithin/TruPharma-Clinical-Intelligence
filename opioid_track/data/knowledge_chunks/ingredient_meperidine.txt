PHARMACOLOGY: MEPERIDINE

Identifiers: RxCUI=6754, ChEMBL=CHEMBL607, PubChem CID=4058
Molecular: C15H21NO2, MW=247.33

Meperidine is classified as an opioid based on its pharmacologic class, but detailed receptor binding data is not available.

Half-life: 0.11 hours
Metabolism: Meperidine is metabolized in the liver by hydrolysis to meperidinic acid followed by partial conjugation with glucuronic acid.
