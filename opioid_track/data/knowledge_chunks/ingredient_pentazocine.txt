PHARMACOLOGY: PENTAZOCINE

Identifiers: RxCUI=8001, ChEMBL=CHEMBL100116, PubChem CID=441278
Molecular: C19H27NO, MW=285.4

Receptor Binding Affinities:
  - mu: Ki = 2.7 nM (source: ChEMBL)
  - kappa: Ki = 7.6 nM (source: ChEMBL)
  - delta: Ki = 62.0 nM (source: ChEMBL)

Potency vs morphine: 0.12x

Pentazocine is classified as an opioid because it acts as a full agonist at the mu opioid receptor (OPRM1) with Ki = 2.7 nM. Its primary opioid effects — analgesia, euphoria, respiratory depression, and physical dependence — are mediated through mu receptor activation.

Half-life: 2.5 hours
Metabolism: Hepatic
  Danger level: Unknown
