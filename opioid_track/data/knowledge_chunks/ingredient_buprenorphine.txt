Buprenorphine is classified as an opioid because it acts as a full agonist at the kappa opioid receptor (OPRK1) with Ki = 0.0 nM. Mu opioid receptor agonist. Its primary opioid effects — dysphoria, sedation, and spinal analgesia — are mediated through kappa receptor activation.

Half-life: 2.77 hours
Metabolism: Buprenorphine is metabolized to norbuprenorphine via Cytochrome P450 3A4/3A5-mediated N-dealkylation.
