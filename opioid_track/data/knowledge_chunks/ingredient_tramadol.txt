Tramadol is classified as an opioid because it acts as a full agonist at the delta opioid receptor (OPRD1) with Ki = 9.4 nM. Its primary opioid effects — anxiolysis, mild analgesia, and convulsant activity — are mediated through delta receptor activation.

Half-life: 5.5 hours
