PHARMACOLOGY: METHADONE

Identifiers: RxCUI=6813, ChEMBL=CHEMBL651, PubChem CID=4095
Molecular: C21H27NO, MW=309.4

Receptor Binding Affinities:
  - mu: Ki = 13.0 nM (source: ChEMBL)

Potency vs morphine: 0.03x

Methadone is classified as an opioid because it acts as a full agonist at the mu opioid receptor (OPRM1) with Ki = 13.0 nM. Its primary opioid effects — analgesia, euphoria, respiratory depression, and physical dependence — are mediated through mu receptor activation.

Half-life: 111.0 hours
